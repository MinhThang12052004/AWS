# **WEEK 9 WORKLOG**

### **Week 9 Objectives**

* Master **AWS IAM** advanced features: policies, roles, and cross-account access.
* Learn **AWS Secrets Manager** and **AWS Systems Manager Parameter Store** for managing sensitive data.
* Implement **AWS KMS** (Key Management Service) for encryption key management.
* Monitor security and compliance with **CloudTrail** and **Config**.
* Practice security best practices: least privilege, MFA, encryption, and audit logging.
